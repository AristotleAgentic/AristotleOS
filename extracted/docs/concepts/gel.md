# Governance Evidence Ledger

The Governance Evidence Ledger is execution accountability infrastructure.

It is not ordinary logging. GEL records bind decision, action hash, policy hash, authority hash, warrant id, witness set, and replay material into a tamper-evident lineage.

It prevents unverifiable post-hoc claims about why an autonomous system acted.

Developers use GEL to audit, replay, export, and explain governed execution.
