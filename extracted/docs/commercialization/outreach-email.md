Hi [Name],

I am Pepper Petersen, founder of AristotleOS. We have built a working proof demo around a simulated 40-UAV swarm operating under network partition and degraded connectivity.

The demo shows a specific governance problem: autonomous systems may need to continue when command links or cloud permissioning are unavailable, but that autonomy cannot become unlimited. In the run, AristotleOS allowed bounded fallback behavior, blocked unauthorized mission expansion, classified stale, revoked, expired, and review-required actions, and produced a post-reconnect reconciliation report with evidence continuity.

We are now looking for one or two UAV, defense, telecom edge, or critical infrastructure partners for a 90-day Disconnected Autonomy Governance Pilot. The goal is not to replace an existing autonomy stack, but to sit upstream of it as the warrant, refusal, evidence, and reconciliation layer for consequential autonomous actions.

I would welcome a short conversation to show the proof demo and see whether one of your autonomous workflows would be a fit for a pilot.
