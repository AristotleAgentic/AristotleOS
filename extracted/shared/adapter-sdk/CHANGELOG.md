## [0.1.0] — 2026-06-05

### Added
- Generic adapter contract. AristotleAdapterTransport, DemonstrationTransport, RecordingTransport, plus three orchestrators (governThroughAdapter for transport-shaped adapters, governThroughResponse for response-shaped adapters, governThroughHandler for handler-shaped framework wrappers).

### Changed
- Initial published surface area; no prior shape to break.
