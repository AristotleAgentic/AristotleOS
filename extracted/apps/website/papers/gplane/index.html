The G-Plane Architecture

Governance Infrastructure for Autonomous Systems. The book-length architecture manuscript and the companion technical paper on runtime warrants, commit gates, and evidence.

AUTHORJ. D. "Pepper" Petersen
EDITIONWards and Warrants
LENGTH541 pages

This revised manuscript develops the wider architecture behind Aristotle Agentic: governance infrastructure for autonomous systems operating in telecommunications, logistics, financial systems, robotics, distributed computing, and other infrastructure environments.

The edition includes the Wards and Warrants model: a structured authority chain from Meta Authority Envelopes and Wards through Authority Domains, runtime registers, Warrants, commit gates, physical invariant gating, and evidence ledgers.

Core contribution: governance is not a values wrapper. It is infrastructure that authorizes action before it becomes real.
